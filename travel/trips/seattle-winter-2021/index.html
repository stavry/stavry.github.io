Seattle (Winter 2021)

2021-12-08
1 minute read
trip report
travel

08.12.21
It’s an overcast day in the Bay, rare for any time of year. We depart early morning from Palo Alto, headed for the San Jose airport. The flight is uneventful save for the descent - the plane shoots north past Seattle (did we get on the wrong flight? I don’t want to go to Canada), turning around eventually in a large arc before descending over the city into SeaTac. We had planned to take an Uber from the airport into the city, but as it turns out, the monorail (Link) is much cheaper ($2!), so we opt for that instead, meandering lazily through the Seattle suburbs with stops few and far between.

We arrive in Capitol Hill a few hours before check-in - time to explore. It’s a good thing we chose to travel light (one Gregory day pack each - a holdover from the Half Dome hike), since we’re on our feet for the next few hours. The first stop is Tacos Chukis.
